üü¢ CAET Entry-Level ‚Äî 8 Exam Categories
100 questions ¬∑ 90 minutes ¬∑ 70% passing ¬∑ Remote proctored via USI
Category 1
Maintenance Procedures & Regulatory Requirements
FAA regulations, maintenance documentation, airworthiness directives
Foundation
Covers 14 CFR Part 43, 91, 145 regulatory knowledge. Technical data usage, maintenance records, AD compliance. Maps directly to RTI Phase 1 & 3 content.
Category 2
Basic Electrical Theory & Systems
DC/AC theory, circuit analysis, power systems fundamentals
Foundation
Ohm's Law, Kirchhoff's Laws, capacitance, inductance, transformers, aircraft electrical power generation and distribution. Core prerequisite for all avionics work.
Category 3
CNS ‚Äî Communication, Navigation & Surveillance
Radio systems, nav aids, transponders, ADS-B basics
Systems
VHF/HF comm, VOR/ILS/GPS navigation, Mode S transponders, ADS-B Out/In, TCAS, weather radar fundamentals. Entry-level understanding of integrated CNS.
Category 4
Flight Instrument & Pitot-Static Systems
EFIS, ADCs, pitot-static plumbing, instrument theory
Systems
Altimeters, airspeed indicators, vertical speed, attitude indicators, EFIS/glass cockpit basics, pitot-static system testing and leak checks.
Category 5
Digital Databus Systems
ARINC 429, RS-232, CAN bus, digital system architecture
Digital
Understanding digital data communication between avionics LRUs. ARINC 429/629, MIL-STD-1553, RS-232/422, CAN bus. Label/word formats, data integrity.
Category 6
Aircraft Wiring, Hardware & Installation
Wire types, connectors, harness routing, installation practices
Hands-On
Wire gauge selection, connector types (D-sub, circular, RF), crimping, soldering, wire routing/clamping, shielding, bonding/grounding, STC/supplemental installations.
Category 7
Tools, Test Equipment & Measurement Techniques
DMMs, oscilloscopes, TDRs, ramp test equipment
Hands-On
Proper use of multimeters, oscilloscopes, signal generators, ramp test sets, TDRs. Measurement techniques, calibration awareness, equipment care.
Category 8
Safety, Housekeeping & Best Practices
ESD, FOD prevention, workspace management, safety protocols
Culture
Electrostatic discharge protection, FOD awareness, hazardous materials handling, lockout/tagout, human factors, professional standards, documentation habits.
